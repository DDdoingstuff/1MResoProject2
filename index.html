My August Resolutions

Escaping the Machine. 30 days at a time.

Create 1 Web Page per Day

Through out the month of August I'm going to create a single webpage per day by tutorial instructions, creative visual ideas that come to me, or maybe by recreating web pages from previous clients that were designed on Wix or Wordpress. I think it's important for me to set small goals like this while learning so I come across a variety of visual layouts to research and execute as I go.

My August Resolution by DD Lattino

Spend at least 30min per day on MIMO App

MIMO has been a great tool for me to use as a complete beginner to coding. I think it might be for 4 year olds, but it actually has been super encouraging since the app tracks your streaks and places you on a competitive board, which has motivated me to stay in first place every week since I started using it about 3 weeks ago.

Create a Visual Animation on the Weekends

To continue growing as a visual and motion graphic artist I will find an interesting animation loop, learn new techniques, and recreate as a fun project once per weekend.
